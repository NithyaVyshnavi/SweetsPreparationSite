Kaju Barfi

Kaju Barfi, also known as Kaju Katli, is a smooth, diamond-shaped Indian sweet made primarily from cashew nuts and sugar. Known for its rich, melt-in-the-mouth texture and elegant silver leaf garnish, it's a festive favorite across India.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashew nuts (kaju)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp ghee (for greasing)
  • Silver varak (optional, for garnish)

Preparation Steps

  1. Grind cashew nuts into a fine powder using a dry mixer grinder (do not over-blend).
  2. In a pan, mix sugar and water and heat until the sugar completely dissolves and a one-string syrup is formed.
  3. Add the cashew powder to the syrup and stir continuously on low flame.
  4. Cook until the mixture starts leaving the sides and forms a soft dough consistency.
  5. Add cardamom powder if using, and mix well.
  6. Transfer the mixture onto a greased surface or parchment paper and knead slightly once it cools enough to handle.
  7. Roll it into a flat sheet and apply silver varak on top (optional).
  8. Cut into diamond shapes and allow it to set.
  9. Store in an airtight container.
