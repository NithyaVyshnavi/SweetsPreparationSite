Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a delicious Indian dessert made from milk solids, deep-fried until golden brown, and soaked in sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup milk (adjust as needed)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1.5 cups water
  • Cardamom pods (4–5)
  • Rose water (optional)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying

Preparation Steps

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee in a bowl.
  2. Gradually add milk to make a soft dough. Rest for 10 minutes.
  3. In a pan, boil sugar and water with cardamom to make syrup. Add rose water if desired.
  4. Make small balls from the dough without cracks.
  5. Heat oil/ghee and fry the balls on medium flame until golden brown.
  6. Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for 2–3 hours.
  7. Serve warm or chilled!
